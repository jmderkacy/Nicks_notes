My name is Nickalas Reynolds. I am a second yea graduate assistant at the University of Oklahoma. I spend most of my time analyzing data retrieved by ALMA and working on the Sooner Radio Telescope hosted by the University of Oklahoma on top of Nielsen Hall.

Research

My current research focuses on the formation and evolution of YSOs and understanding the nature of stellar multiplicities and formation of proto-stellar disks. We focus on dense star forming regions like that of Perseus and Orion.
